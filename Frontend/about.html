About JobTrack

JobTrack is a simple web application built to help students, graduates, and job seekers organize their job and internship applications without relying on messy spreadsheets.

Get started free Back to home

Our purpose

Making the job search easier to manage

🎯 Built for job seekers

JobTrack gives users one place to save applications, track statuses, and stay focused during the job or internship search process.

📊 Clear organization

Instead of using scattered notes or spreadsheets, users can view their applications in a clean dashboard with simple status labels.

📅 Better planning

The app helps users keep up with interviews, deadlines, and application progress so important opportunities are easier to manage.

💡 Student-built project

JobTrack was created as a course project by students in CSC 5750 at Wayne State University.
