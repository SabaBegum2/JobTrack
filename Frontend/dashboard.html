Quick Tips

Tailor every application

Customize your resume and cover letter for each role. Mirror keywords from the job description to pass ATS filters.

Prepare your STAR stories

For behavioral interviews, have 5–6 stories ready using Situation, Task, Action, Result format.

Follow up after applying

Send a polite follow-up email 5–7 days after applying. It shows initiative and keeps you top of mind.
